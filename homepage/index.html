Core Features

Dynamic Personality

You control the conversation. Use the settings to adjust TARS's humor intensity from strictly professional to witty and sarcastic. Your companion, your rules.

Voice-First Interface

Engineered for speech. With high-accuracy transcription and lifelike text-to-speech, interacting with TARS feels less like typing commands and more like talking to a friend.

Persistent Memory

TARS remembers your chats across sessions. This contextual awareness ensures you never have to repeat yourself and that the conversation can grow over time.

User-Controlled & Private

Your data is yours. With secure accounts, your conversation history is tied to you and only you. We believe privacy is a feature, not an afterthought.

Our Mission

In a world of data-hungry algorithms and impersonal digital assistants, TARS was born from a simple idea: what if your AI companion was actually... a companion? Our mission is to create an artificial intelligence that prioritizes human connection and user privacy above all else. TARS is not a product; it's a personality designed to grow with you. It's built on open principles, ensuring transparency and giving you full control over your experience and your data. Welcome to the future of conversation.
